Our Story

Our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision: to make veterinary care more accessible and convenient for pet parents. We envisioned a platform that could connect a vet with a pet parent within minutes, providing assistance for daily pet care needs without the need for constant in person visits.

Our commitment includes offering affordable pricing, ensuring that quality care is within reach for every pet. Beyond individual pet owners, we also sought to provide breeders with a comprehensive pet care solution for their clients, offering peace of mind and 24/7 access to veterinary support for client’s pets.

At PawsToTail, we're rewriting the story of pet care - where convenience, affordability, and peace of mind come together. The unlimited consultations with Veterinarians are FREE with a membership in PawsToTail!

Why Choose PawsToTail?

Choose PawsToTail for a pet care experience that puts your furry friend's well-being first. Our commitment to quality veterinary care, accessible 24/7 from anywhere, ensures peace of mind for both pet parents and breeders. We understand the value of your time, offering convenience and stress-free access to our dedicated team of experienced veterinarians. Plus our members save time and money by avoiding unnecessary in person Vet visits.

Our service goes beyond routine check-ups, addressing specific health concerns with compassion and expertise. By choosing PawsToTail, you're joining a community of pet lovers dedicated to ensuring the happiness and health of your cherished companions. Trust us to provide the best care for your pets, because they deserve nothing less.

Who do we serve?

We proudly serve a diverse range of pet enthusiasts, including pet parents seeking convenient and affordable veterinary care, individuals looking for swift and practical solutions to daily pet care needs, and responsible breeders desiring comprehensive Veterinarian support for their clients pet.

Our most common clients are those who value accessibility, affordability, and a commitment to their pets' well-being, making us the go-to choice for pet care that goes beyond the ordinary.
